ID: A unique identifier for each cricket match

City: The city where the match took place, providing geographical context for the event.

Date: The date of the match

Season: The season of the cricket tournament, providing information about the specific edition of the tournament.

MatchNumber: The match number within the season and also describes type of matches like Final
Qualifier 2
Eliminator
Qualifier 1

if they are of playoffs type.

Team1: The name of the first team playing in the match.

Team2: The name of the second team playing in the match.

Venue: The name of the stadium where the match was held.

TossWinner: The name of team winning the toss

TossDecision: The decision made by the toss-winning team which can be bat or field.

SuperOver: Whether a Super Over was played to break a tie, Y if played , N if super over is not player.

WinningTeam: The name of team that won the that match.

WonBy: Describes how the match was won by Runs,Wickets, SuperOver or NoResult

Margin: The margin of victory, if corresponding WonBy cell is "Runs" then no. of runs would mentioned, if corresponding Wonby cell is "Wickets" then no. of wickets by which winning team won would be monetioned.

Method: The method of winning either D/L or NA , if there is rain then D/L method is used , otherwise NA which describes normal match

Player_of_Match: The name of Man of the Match player 

Team1Players: A list of players in Team 1, providing information about the team composition. Example - ["Player1","Player2","Player3","Player4","Player5","Player6","Player7","Player8","Player9","Player10","Player11"]

Team2Players: A list of players in Team 2, similar example as Team1Players column

Umpire1: The name of first umpire officiating the match.

Umpire2: The name of second umpire officiating the match
